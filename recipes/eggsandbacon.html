Eggs and Bacon

Description

Perfect breakfast after working out

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Break the eggs into a deep plate and scramble them
  2. Add salt
  3. Chop onion into tiny pieces
  4. Pour a little bit of oil into the pan
  5. Put bacon into the pan and wait untill it gets darker
  6. Take the bacon out and add the onions to the pan
  7. Wait until the onions get browner
  8. Add the eggs and mix them with the onions
  9. Eat
